TV Screen Technologies in Australia

Issue:

Many Australian shoppers feel overwhelmed by the technical jargon of the market, making it difficult to tell which screen technologies are actually mainstream.

Demonstrate Issue:

Without a clear sense of what is standard, consumers often struggle to choose between competing terms like LCD, LCD(LED), and OLED.

Idea for overcoming issue:

Cut through the confusion by analyzing the official government registry to see which technology type appears most frequently

Describe the idea:

The goal was to pinpoint the most prevalent technology so that buyers know exactly what to expect when browsing the local market.

Show before & after:

According to the dataset, it shows LCD, LCD (LED), and OLED are available, but LCD (LED) is the most frequent technology used in modern TVs.

Recommendation:

To ensure you have the widest range of models and price points to choose from, we suggest focusing your search on LCD (LED) models.
